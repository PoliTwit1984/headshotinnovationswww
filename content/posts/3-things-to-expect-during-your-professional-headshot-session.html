As a business professional in St. Louis, your headshot is often the first impression you make on potential clients and colleagues. Understanding what happens during your session will help you feel prepared and confident. Here's what you can expect when you step into our professional studio.

1. Expert Direction and Coaching

Unlike typical photo sessions, your professional headshot experience includes continuous guidance from a Peter Hurley trained photographer. You'll receive clear direction on:

  • Optimal posture for a confident, approachable look
  • Facial expression techniques that convey professionalism
  • Head positioning to capture your best angles

During the entire session, we'll guide you through proven techniques that help showcase your best professional image. Even if you typically don't feel comfortable in front of the camera, our expert coaching ensures natural, confident results.

2. Real-Time Image Review

Using professional tethered shooting technology, you'll see your images on a large screen as we shoot. This immediate feedback allows us to:

  • Make real-time adjustments to achieve your desired look
  • Ensure your expression aligns with your professional brand
  • Select your best shots during the session

This instant review process helps you understand how small adjustments can make a big difference in your professional appearance. You'll be actively involved in creating your perfect headshot, ensuring the final images align with your vision.

3. A Relaxed, Professional Environment

Many clients begin their session feeling nervous about being photographed. Our approach creates a comfortable, confidence-building environment where you can:

  • Relax and be yourself while maintaining professionalism
  • Try different expressions and poses without pressure
  • Receive encouraging feedback throughout the session

Our studio environment is designed to help you feel at ease while maintaining a professional atmosphere. The session is paced to allow time for adjustments and ensure we capture authentic expressions that reflect your professional persona.
