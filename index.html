Headshot Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't think I'm photogenic?

As a St. Louis headshot photographer, I believe that being photogenic isn't solely about physical appearance, but rather about the confidence and self-assurance that shines through. Every individual has the ability to shine in photos. The key is to find a skilled St. Louis photographer who can help you overcome any self-doubts and bring out your best self.

How to select a St. Louis headshot photographer?

While it's important to admire a St. Louis headshot photographer's portfolio, it's equally crucial to establish a connection with the photographer. Building rapport is essential as it can impact your comfort level and the authenticity of your headshots. Choose a St. Louis photographer who makes you feel understood and confident in front of the camera.

How will I guide you during your headshot session?

As your St. Louis headshot photographer, my goal is to create a relaxed environment where you can truly be yourself. I'll provide tailored guidance and even sprinkle in some of my quirky humor to help divert your attention from the lens. This will allow your natural and spontaneous expressions to shine through.

Will you retouch my headshots?

You can trust me to enhance your St. Louis headshots by retouching them. I prioritize maintaining a natural look by using subtle adjustments. During our headshot session in St. Louis, we will discuss your specific preferences for retouching, ensuring that the final images are customized to your liking.

How long does it take to get my headshots?

Following our headshot session in St. Louis, the comprehensive retouching process usually takes up to 5 business days. For St. Louis clients who require expedited service, we offer a same-day retouching option at a cost of $50 per image, ensuring that your urgent needs are met with the utmost quality and efficiency.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We offer a variety of payment options for our St. Louis clients. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express, PayPal, Square, and Venmo. Additionally, traditional payment methods such as checks and cash are also accepted, ensuring a hassle-free and accessible experience when booking your headshot session in St. Louis.
